Andiamo was a song released by Michael e in 2015. It was a chillout track with a lot of potential for a remix, so I added a 90's Atmospheric DnB twist to it.

After sending the track to the original artist for permission to release it, he liked it and even offered to release it as a bonus track in his next album!

The version on this page is different from the release, as it has a fade-in at the beginning that he wanted to replace with the chords from the original track.

The vocals at the beginning are the same used in my track Faceplant, and the vocals you hear throughout are taken directly from Cigarettes Out The Window by TV Girl.
