Faceplant

by Cactile
Released 25th Apr 2021

0:00 / 0:00


Click here to download!

Faceplant is a combination of various different samples and loops from different places, making what I consider to be a semi-decent DnB track. This is my best album art to-date.

The drop, buildup and the part after the drop come from samples I found on looperman.com.

The part where you hear people talking and laughing comes from a video my secondary school friends made of them playing GTA V. This song was made with them in mind.

In my honest opinion, I could've mixed this better and I'll likely remake it in the future. The bass is muddy in some parts.
