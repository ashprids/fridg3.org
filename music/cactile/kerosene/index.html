Kerosene is the second song in the Kerosene EP. It's also the name of the fuel used by most aircraft. Compared to the first, this one sounds more serious.

I made this song when I started listening to "breakcore" (or whatever you wanna call the atmospheric DNB genre).

This song was heavily inspired by Ricecakes by The Virus and Antidote, and the beginning is even a sample from it. I also used its lead melody in a different key.

The melody at 2:16 is from Triage at Dawn by Valve.
