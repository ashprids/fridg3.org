Click here to download!

Baphomet is the first song in the Kerosene EP. It's also the name of a deity that the Knights Templar were accused of worshipping.

Both this song and Kerosene use the same atmospheric pad, but it's less noticable in Kerosene because it's overlayed with another.

It might seem weird to put this song and Kerosene in the same EP, but I quite like how opposite of each other they are, and consider that enough of a reason to put them together. Opposites attract, right?
