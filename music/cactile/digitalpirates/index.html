Digital Pirates

by Cactile
Released 2nd Jan 2020

0:00 / 0:00


Click here to download!

Digital Pirates was the first song I've ever distributed to streaming platforms. It was also the first song I've fully made without using mostly third-party loops, as before this I was using loops from MusicMakerJam (and no, those projects will NOT see the light of day).

The name was inspired by Dreaming in Digital by Digital Insanity, a song exclusively heard in the Digital Insanity keygen used for pirating Sony Vegas Pro 13.

Most of what you hear comes from the FL Studio 20 stock plugins, with the exception of the drop drums, which comes from a sample sharing website. I'm not able to find the original sample.

The lead you hear between drops was inspired by Omen (Reprise) by The Prodigy.
