Music You Don't Like

by Cactile
Released 25th Apr 2021

0:00 / 0:00


Click here to download!

Music You Don't like is a highly experimental combination of various different melodies that I've developed up to its creation.

After showing someone my songs, they said I should make another. After telling them I had no ideas for a full one, they suggested just making one with random noises, so this song was born.

The part starting at 1:57 uses the same melody as Girl In The Fire by Pendulum, one of my favourite songs from them.

The drums starting at 1:21 were taken from Dreaming in Digital by Digital Insanity.
