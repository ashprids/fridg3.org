I make music btw

10 | 27th Dec 2024

Music is awesome. It seems weird to say that there was a time, not long ago, when music wasn't a big part of the human life. But in the modern day, it can not only be heard everywhere, but it now also influences our emotions, opinions and maybe even our actions.

Music is very likely to be the only form of media that could outlive the rest. In the fall of humanity as a result of apocalypse, music would still remain as prevalent as it is today. No other form of media could say the same.

I feel quite close to the music I listen to, I view it as art. I think it's only human nature to see something amazing, and want to replicate it.

So, I make music. I've been making music for a few years now, and I've learnt a lot about the processes and compositions of songs, and how they can be used to convey emotions and stories.

..I mean, I say that, but in reality I'm just making music because it sounds good. And that's really all music needs to be, in my opinion. There are only so many emotions; if you focus too much on your song's meaning, you'll end up creating the same song we've heard a million times before. See Taylor Swift.

Music means MORE to me than emotion. I enjoy music the most when the melody is so angelic it gives me goosebumps. I enjoy music the most when the beat is so catchy it makes me want to dance. I enjoy music the most when the lyrics are treated as an instrument, rather than the instruments being a backdrop to the lyrics.

But enough about all that, you're CLEARLY here to listen to MY music. After all, this is fridg3.org! You're here for ME!
