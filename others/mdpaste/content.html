mdpaste

mdpaste is a tool for quickly pasting markdown content and sharing it with others. it's simple; paste your markdown into the textbox or upload a file, press "generate link" and share the result!

you may also choose to use a password. your file will be securely encrypted using that password, and will be inaccessible to anyone without it, even site administrators and developers.

all content is stored in the UK (London) and is automatically deleted after 30 days.

your formatted markdown will appear here automatically.
